Who We Are

Saskatchewan Roots,
Prairie-Tough Quality

lmentals has been serving the prairies with reliable, quality-cut lumber products. We operate out of southern Saskatchewan and serve customers across Alberta, providing everything from standard framing lumber to specialty custom cuts.
